Campus Crusade at NAU Page

The NAU chapter of Campus Crusade for Christ needed a dynamic web solution that leaders could easily use to communicate with the rest of the members.

I launched this site to meet that need. Essentially, I created a 100% custom template design for Wordpress. This was no easy task as it involved a lot more coding that I had originally anticipated, but it certainly displays my HTML/PHP/CSS skills.

The live site is located at nau.edu/campuscrusade.
