Skate Rock

It has been a dream of mine for a long time to play a rock show with some of my favorite bands. This poster is an expression of that idea.

For this poster, my old band, The Honors Project gets to play an imaginary show at a skate park with some of the bands that inspired us.

I took a couple of skateboarding pictures from some old magazines I had and hand-traced some images into Illustrator. I think this really captures the haphazardness of skateboarding and good ol' rock music.
