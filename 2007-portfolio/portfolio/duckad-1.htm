Break The Mold

This design evolved out of an ad I created for the back cover of my fictional magazine, Art of Play. Originally, it was for an imaginary toy design school, but I liked the concept so much that I decided to play with it some more, and finished with this.

The concept is that in order to rise above, you must dare to be different. The tagline, "Break the mold" is a play-on-words that came from the original design. In order to be mass-produced, toys are molded. So, if you want something new, you need to "Break the mold".
