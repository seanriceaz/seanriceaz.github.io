Jitters

The full name of this conceptual company is Jitters Caffeinated Fruit Juices. The company needed a mark that would (hypothetically) go on their product packaging.

The idea behind Jitters is the shakiness you get when you consume too much caffeine. I combined that with an image of an apple. Theoretically, Jitters could have a different fruit in their mark for each different flavor of juice.
