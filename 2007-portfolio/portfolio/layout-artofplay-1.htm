Art Of Play Magazine

This is a mock magazine that I designed for a class project. My idea was to make a magazine targeted at people interested in designing toys. No such magazine (to my knowledge) currently exists.

Realistically, this magazine would probably have such a small readership that it wouldn't make it very far, but I wanted to try something new.

This magazine showcases some of my publication design skills. It was good to have the freedom to experiment with image and text arrangement through this project.

All of the content in this magazine, except for some images in the Table of Contents and one image in the article were created or photographed by me.
