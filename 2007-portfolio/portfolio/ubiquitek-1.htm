Ubiquitek

I created this product and packaging design for a futuristic item called the Ubiquitek Neural Interface Device. The product, meant to exist about 30 years in the future, is a media player that plugs directly into the user's brain.

The concept for the ad is "future retro". I wanted to compare the new product with older technology. So, I referenced the Apple iPod ads.

As a side note, I did all the 3d texturing and 3d rendering needed for this piece myself.
